VSA

Vietnamese Student Association at UNC Charlotte

The Vietnamese Student Association at UNC-Charlotte was established in 1989. It's primary purposes are to: - To promote the aspects of culture and tradition of Vietnam towards interested students and at the same time strengthen and perpetuate the unity of students at UNCC. - To extend friendship to other clubs and organizations. - To exchange knowledge and help students within our club succeed in education. - To assist and support personal improvement as well as the surrounding Charlotte communities. VSA encourages our members to use academic abilities from their majors to aid VSA in their activities and events. The Vietnamese Student Association does not discriminate on the basis of sex, religion, sexual orientation, physical ability, or national origin.
